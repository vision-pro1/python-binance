python-binance

The python-binance library provides a simple and intuitive interface for interacting with the Binance API. It allows you to place trades, check order status, and retrieve market data. The library is designed to be easy to use and provides a wide range of features, making it a great choice for anyone looking to trade on Binance. With its powerful and flexible API, the python-binance library is the perfect tool for anyone looking to automate their trading experiences.
